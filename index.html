Since October 2017, I'm a Research Scientist with CNRS, affiliated to LORIA. Before that I was a Postdoc at University of Copenhagen within the CoAStaL team. I received a PhD in 2015 from the University Paris 7, where I also did my Master. You can find my CV in English here and in French here.

Last news

June, 2019: Our system ToNy won the DisRPT shared task on multilingual discourse segmentation across formalisms! Please visit the DisRPT page, and find the paper here and the code here.

Oct 1st, 2018: Charlotte Roze joins the team as a postdoc, to work on multi-lingual discourse parsing, and cross-formalism analysis (thanks to an LUE grant + LORIA grant).

July 30, 2017: I'm co-organizing the first workshop for Women and Underrepresented Groups in NLP co-located with ACL, Vancouver. More info on the website: WiNLP

Domains of interest

Natural language processing, discourse parsing, machine learning, domain adaptation, cross-lingual and cross-domain learning.
